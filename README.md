# 🎰 Roulette Game

The Roulette Game is an exciting and interactive betting game where players can place bets and watch as the wheel spins to determine the outcome. Test your luck and strategy to win big!

## 🕹️ How to Play?

### Increasing Your Bet:
- Press the **Space key** to increase your bet amount.
- Each press increases your bet by **$100**.
- The updated bet amount will be displayed on the screen.

### Starting the Game:
- To begin, simply **click on the color** you want from the options displayed on the screen.

## 🏆 Winning Conditions
- The game outcome is determined by the spinning roulette wheel.
- If the wheel stops on your chosen color or number, you win based on the payout odds.

## 🎨 Visuals
- The game includes a clean interface with a roulette wheel and clear betting options.
- Watch the animation as the wheel spins and the ball lands on a winning spot.
